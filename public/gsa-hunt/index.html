HOW IT WORKS

Enter your Discord username once — your progress is saved in your browser and synced to the shared leaderboard so the whole community can see standings in real time.

Tap any task to mark it complete. Your score updates immediately. Check the Leaderboard tab to see where you rank against other GSA members.

Tasks are worth 5–20 points each. The bonus task (be recognized by a streamer) is worth 20 extra points. Maximum score: 125 points.
